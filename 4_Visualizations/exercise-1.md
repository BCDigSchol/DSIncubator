## Critique a visualization project

Form pairs, select a visualization project, explore it together, and discuss the questions below. Each pair will provide a summary of their critique to the group.

## Visualization Projects
- Ellis Act Eviction Map
- Forced Migration of Enslaved People
- Mapping Prohibition
- The Growth of Newspapers Across the U.S.: 1690-2011 


## Discussion Questions
1. Does this project have a clearly defined message? What is it?
2. What is being visualized? 
3. What are some of the applied tools or methods?
4. Does the visualization add value or impact and how so?
5. What kind of data are they using? What sources is it drawn from?
6. Who are the collaborators, project team members, or other partners on this project?
