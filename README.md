# Fall 2017 Boston College Digital Scholarship Incubator

Welcome to the Digital Scholarship Incubator!

## Overview

We will have 7 sessions together, each two hours long.

0. [Introduction](0_Intro) - August 28
1. [Humanities & Social Science Data](1_Data) - September 11 (preliminary reflection due)
2. [Thematic Digital Collections](2_Collections) - September 18 (proposal overview due)
3. [Digital Editions](3_Editions) - September 25
4. [Visualizing Data with Maps & Timelines](4_Visualizations) - October 2 (proposal work plan & infrastructure due)
5. [Text Analysis](5_Analysis) - October 16 (revised proposal due)
6. [Crit Session](6_Crit_Session) - October 30 (final reflection due)

During the incubator, we will also ask that you document your participation through [reflections](Reflections).
